Hybrid Solutions

Voice-centric solutions are in demand by customers in verticals such as: Retail, Healthcare, Hospitality, Government, Manufacturing, etc. These types of customers require highly integrated voice solutions that cannot be delivered through vanilla SIP products.E-Metro Tel software runs on our appliances, in the UCX cloud, or in a customer’s virtualized environment such as VMware. The E-MetroTel software is feature-rich in capabilities such as shared line appearance, zero touch phone programming and many other features not available through “SIP-only” solutions.

E-MetroTel XSTIM

Smarter than SIP signalling.The proprietary XSTIM technology allows for advanced signalling between the server and devices We support advanced features that are unavailable on SIP like:
Hot Desking – Easily move your telephone programming from one device to another.
Shared Call Appearance – Have calls ring on and be answered by multiple devices.
Provisioning – Touchless and remote provisioning of our Infinity 5000 product line.
E911 Notification – option to notify others in the organization by phone or email when anybody dials 911
